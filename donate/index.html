Laptop Donations

Thank you for your interest to give your laptop a second life! 

As a small non-profit with limited staff and storage space, we have partnered with SingTel to collect used personal laptops and smartphones at four Singtel shop locations. Our E-Waste Recycling partner will sort through and categorise the devices for refurbishing, recycling, and proper disposal.

If your personal laptop is broken or obsolete, we encourage you to responsibly recycle at e-waste bins these various locations.

If your organisation would like to contribute your decommissioned laptops to benefit disadvantaged communities, please email contactus@engineeringgood.org

EG provides used business-grade laptops to our end-users, as they tend to offer satisfactory user experience, be more durable and easily serviceable by us and the recipients. This helps to best serve our mission, in providing appropriate solutions for the community!

Terms of Donation: By donating devices and/or accessories to Engineering Good, you agree to transfer full ownership of these items to Engineering Good (“EG”). EG reserves the right to, at its sole discretion, refurbish, distribute, repurpose, recycle, e-waste or realise the residual value of donated devices and/or accessories that subsidise its operational costs in order to execute its charitable mission to the fullest extent.
