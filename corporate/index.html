Our Partners

Reduce E-Waste With Our Buyback Programme

Revive Your Old Work Laptops for a Meaningful Cause.

Don’t let dusty work devices go to waste—give them renewed purpose! Our buyback programme lets you reclaim value from decommissioned laptops and reduces electronic waste.

Engineering Good will install Windows 11 and essential drivers. We ensure a hassle-free experience for your staff to repurchase their former work laptops for personal use.

We charge a nominal fee which directly supports Engineering Good’s programmes that uplift vulnerable communities. Get in touch with us at contactus@engineeringgood.org

Volunteer With Your Team

join our corporate volunteering Workshops.

Forge unbreakable bonds with your co-workers through shared laughter, peculiar challenges, and perhaps a touch of mild embarrassment—all for a cause.

Discover the art of meticulous laptop inspection and the wonders of automation to bring life back into digital black and white boxes. Let’s turn used laptops into sparkly gifts for vulnerable communities! 

Write in to: contactus@engineeringgood.org and request for a quote today!
