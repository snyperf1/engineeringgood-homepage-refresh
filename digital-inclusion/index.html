Digital Inclusion

7 in 10 rental flat households in Singapore lack computer or tablet access. In contrast, Singapore generates 60,000 tonnes of e-waste annually (equivalent to 70 mobile phones per person)!

We’ve salvaged and distributed over 9,000 laptops, via 200+ partner social service agencies, to disadvantaged communities.
