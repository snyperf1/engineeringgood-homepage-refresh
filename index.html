About

Building an inclusive world with practical engineering.

Engineering Good brings together staff, volunteers, donors, sponsors, and supporters with skills in technology, engineering, and other fields to serve disadvantaged communities in Singapore.

The team refurbishes donated laptops, reduces e-waste, and creates affordable open-source assistive devices for people with disabilities.
