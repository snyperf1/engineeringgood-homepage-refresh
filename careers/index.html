Join us in bridging the digital divide

We at Engineering Good are fun and unapologetic rulebreakers that move forward despite what the world throws in our way, and do it with a smile on our face that will probably give us wrinkles later in life.

Before reading further you might want to know more about our un-corporate culture. Please have a read before applying to see if you’d REALLY like to join our family. Because we’re like Hotel California – you can check out but you don’t really leave!

Our un-corporate Culture

Unity and Teamwork

We work for the good of the beneficiaries.

diversity And empathy

for people who think and act differently than you.

Open-Mindedness and Respectful Discussions

Talk politics and religion all you like, but treat others with respect. Even if they choose to call 3-in-1 Indocafe “coffee”. Or prefer Apple products.

Humility and Collaboration

Take pride in our work but know that we build things that will be built upon to build better-er things. We stand on the shoulders of giants.

Curiosity and Open Communication

So we might tend to be a bit more forward – please don’t take it the wrong way macam like entitled people. We just want to get things done and not waste time tiptoe-ing around sensitivities.

Take Initiative and Ownership

We are here because we see a need. EG exists to give you the platform and the resources. If you see something that needs doing, go do it. Ask for forgiveness later.

Cat Lover

I mean, you can join us, but… we have a cat.
